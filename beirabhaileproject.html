Beir Abhaile ár Marbh

Beir Abhaile ár Marbh (Bring Home our Dead) was awarded the overall prize in the Municipal Gallery, dlr Lexicon, Open Exhibition, entitled Utopia/Dystopia. The exhibition was selected by Aoife Ruane, Director of Highlanes Gallery, 13 December 2019 - 26 January 2020.

The work was also exhibited at Signal Arts Centre 30 Years of Making at the Mermaid Arts Centre, 29 March - 24th April 2021.
